Pack information

Trained adult leaders of the Pack 630 volunteer their time to provide safe environment for youths (boys and girls) to experience Scouting.

Pack holds montly Pack meetings at Anderson Elementary cafeteria at 7pm-8pm every 3rd Thursday of a month (with some adjustments during winter and summer).

Pack accepts children of all faiths and schools, however the Pack has two school assigned - Anderson Elementary and Story Elementary

Contact the Pack leaders for more information.
